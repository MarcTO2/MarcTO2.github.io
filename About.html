LIFE OF MUHAMMAD ALI

Muhammad ali, also known as Cassius clay before he converted into being a muslim was born on the 17th of January 1972. He was nicknamed the 'Greatest' cause he dominated the whole heavyweight class. Ali was born and raised in Louisville, Kentucky and he began training at the age of 12. By the age of 18 years old he won a gold medal in the light heavyweight division at the 1960 Summer olympics and turned professional later that year. He became a Muslim after 1961 and won the world heavyweight championship, defeating Sonny Liston in a major upset on February 25, 1964, at age 22 and that's how he was born into greatness
